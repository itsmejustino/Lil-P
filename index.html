About Me

Hi! My name is Justin Ortiz. Welcome to my Portfolio page. I started my programming and development journey in the Summer of 2020. My reintroduction to computer programming came about after spending my free time during the Pandemic looking for a more flexible and rewarding job to apply my problem-solving skills. Because of this curiosity my interest in programming re-sparked since my last dance with it in Highschool when I learned the fundamentals of C++. My interest turned into a fiery passion when I re-immersed myself into the computer programming world by self-teaching via watching Youtube videos and taking classes on Treehouse Learning. After some time, self-teaching I decided to expand my knowledge further by joining the Denver University Full Stack Programming bootcamp. Through this most recent experience I have furthered my abilities in learning new programming languages that I can apply to real world problems. You will be able to get a great idea of my work by looking through my deployed project quick links listed in the My Projects section of this page.

I started my dedicated programming and development journey in the Summer of 2020. Since I was young I always had an interest in building and deconstructing things. My infatuation with learning and learning how things worked lead me to computer programming. I started spending much of my free time during the Pandemic developing my programming skills on my own. Because of this curiosity my interest in programming re-sparked since my last dance with it in Highschool when I learned the fundamentals of C++.

I re-immersed myself into the computer programming world by self-teaching via watching Youtube videos, building small apps, and taking classes on Treehouse Learning. After some time self-teaching, I decided to expand my knowledge further by joining the Denver University Full Stack Programming Bootcamp.

Through this most recent experience I have furthered my abilities in learning new programming languages that I can apply to real world problems. You will be able to get a great idea of my work by looking through my deployed project quick links listed in the My Projects section of this page.
